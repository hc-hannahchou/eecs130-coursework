About Me

Born into a musical family, Hannah Chou began her violin studies at the age of five in Fremont, California. She is a senior at Northwestern University- Bienen School of Music and her primary teachers include Yuan-Qing Yu, Mikhail Kopelman, and John Chisholm. In her orchestral career, Hannah has served as section principal in the Northwestern School Orchestra and Eastman School Symphony Orchestra. Additionally, she is a Finalist for the New World Symphony and Associate Member of the Civic Orchestra of Chicago. Hannah will be continuing her violin studies in the Masters program at UCLA Herb Albert School of Music and will be the newest member of their graduate string quartet in residence.
